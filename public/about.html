Our Sweet Story

Discover the rich heritage and passion behind Surties Sweet Shop, where tradition meets innovation in every delightful bite.

A Legacy of Sweetness Since 2020

What started as a small sweet shop in Surat has blossomed into a beloved destination for authentic Indian sweets. For over three decades, we've been crafting delicious moments and spreading joy through our traditional recipes.

Each sweet in our collection tells a story - of tradition, of innovation, and of our commitment to quality. We take pride in using the finest ingredients and time-honored techniques passed down through generations.
