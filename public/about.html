Our Journey Through Time

1990

A small sweet shop in Surat with big dreams, we started our journey with traditional Indian sweets made from cherished family recipes.

2000

Growing with Gujarat's love, we expanded to multiple locations while keeping our commitment to quality and tradition alive.

2010

Embracing innovation, we introduced fusion sweets blending Indian traditions with international flavors.

2020

Taking a digital leap, we brought our sweet delights to your doorstep with online ordering and delivery services.
